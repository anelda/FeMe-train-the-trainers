# Workshop Activity Alignment Report

## Activity 1: Mindset Spectrum
- **What participants do:** Position themselves on a growth–fixed spectrum for teaching skills, then discuss with a differently-positioned partner what it would take to shift towards growth.
- **Activity Bloom's level:** Analyse
- **Matched outcome:** Outcome 1 — "Apply a growth mindset to your own development as an educator, not just to your learners."
- **Outcome Bloom's level:** Apply
- **Alignment:** ACTIVITY EXCEEDS OUTCOME

The activity asks participants to examine and classify their beliefs, then compare perspectives — that is Analyse, one level above Apply. This is appropriate stretch for a facilitated workshop. The outcomes map already flags this and suggests levelling up the outcome verb.

## Activity 2: Curse of Knowledge Swap
- **What participants do:** Write a beginner-friendly explanation, receive feedback on confusing parts, and identify assumptions they did not see.
- **Activity Bloom's level:** Analyse
- **Matched outcome:** Outcome 4 — "Analyse how the curse of knowledge shapes your own teaching practice."
- **Outcome Bloom's level:** Analyse
- **Alignment:** MATCH

## Activity 3: Feedback Audit
- **What participants do:** Map feedback sources onto a fast/slow × specific/vague grid, assess gaps, and commit to adding a fast+specific source.
- **Activity Bloom's level:** Evaluate
- **Matched outcome:** Outcome 2 — "Evaluate why your current feedback sources may be too slow or too noisy to effectively guide your teaching."
- **Outcome Bloom's level:** Evaluate
- **Alignment:** MATCH

## Activity 4: Design a Quick Check — Together
- **What participants do:** Collaboratively design a low-stakes check for a concept and plan what to change if learners get it wrong.
- **Activity Bloom's level:** Create
- **Matched outcome:** Outcome 3 — "Design a low-stakes check on understanding that creates a faster feedback loop for both you and your learners."
- **Outcome Bloom's level:** Create
- **Alignment:** MATCH

## Activity 5: Diagnose Before You Prescribe — Role Play
- **What participants do:** Role-play diagnosing where a learner's understanding breaks down, then design a reusable diagnostic protocol based on what they practised.
- **Activity Bloom's level:** Create
- **Matched outcome:** Outcome 5 — "Design a simple diagnostic approach to uncover where a struggling learner's understanding breaks down before choosing an intervention."
- **Outcome Bloom's level:** Create
- **Alignment:** MATCH

*Note: This activity was updated to include a design step (step 6) after the role-play rounds, raising it from Apply to Create.*

All five outcomes have a corresponding workshop activity.
